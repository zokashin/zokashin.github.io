Smart Home Solutions for Large-Scale Deployments

The market for smart home solutions is expected to reach $165 billion by 2025 as hundreds of millions of home owners choose to invest in convenience, property value, and energy management. However, multifamily residential and medium-sized commercial buildings, which are too complex for standard smart home solutions but too small for industrial scale automation, have been conspicuously left out of this technological revolution. As a result, they have failed to modernize and continue to stretch their operating budgets while single-family homes and large-scale commercial properties are realizing the benefits of sophisticated building intelligence.

Zōkashin fills this gap with its smart home-type system designed specifically for large buildings in need of technology-driven management solutions that benefit owners and tenants alike. Whether you want to preserve asset life, improve tenant comfort, manage energy and/or emissions, or integrate workflow, we can help you do so while generating positive ROI.

Platform Features

Centralized & Distributed Controls

Tenants can control their HVAC units from their smartphones, while controls can be also be centralized for commercial deployments.

Scheduling

Residential tenants can control runtime schedules while commercial facilities managers can centralize scheduling while allowing authorized employees to adjust temperatures as needed during business hours.

Fault detection

Our devices will tell you if your HVAC units are behaving abnormally and recommend appropriate courses of action to optimize efficiency and reduce costly equipment failures.

Real Time Data

Our system delivers key operational insights in real time, providing actionable information to decisionmakers.

Maintenance Integration

Predictive, reactive, and user-submitted maintenance requests can be sent directly to your contracted maintenance provider via email or integrated with your current dispatch system.

OEM Integration

We work directly with your HVAC’s manufacturer to ensure your equipment is running at an optimal level.

Utility Integration

Integrate with your utility for better service and Demand Response revenue.

Marketplace

Find new maintenance service providers for long-term relationships, emergency situations, and anything in between.

Ultimate Flexibility

Our system interfaces with virtually any kind of HVAC system, is simple to install, and requires no retrofitting.
